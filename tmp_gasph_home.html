Our Mission & Vision

A simple app to help the community find better gas prices.

Our Mission

To help Filipino drivers save money by sharing gas price information. We think every peso saved matters, and we're trying to make it easier to find cheaper fuel.

Our Vision

To build useful apps that serve our local community well. We hope to create simple solutions that help Filipino drivers and maybe inspire others to build helpful tools too.

Why This Matters

Many Filipino drivers spend time looking for affordable fuel. Gas-ph is our small attempt to help with that. We're just trying to make it a bit easier to find cheaper gas stations in your area.
